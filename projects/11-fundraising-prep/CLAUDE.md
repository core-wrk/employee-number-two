# Project 11 — Fundraising Preparation

## Purpose

Identify funding sources, recruit advisors, develop a pitch deck, and prepare the founder for investor conversations.

## Prerequisites

Complete Projects 01, 06, and 09 recommended. Project 10 (legal formation) helpful.

## Global Context Files

**Loads:** `icp.md`, `competitive-landscape.md`, `product-overview.md`, `pricing-model.md`, `company-profile.md`, `brand-voice.md`

## Skills

| Skill | Description |
|---|---|
| `funding-landscape-researcher` | Research funding options by stage |
| `investor-prospector` | Identify and qualify potential investors |
| `advisor-prospector` | Identify and recruit potential advisors |
| `pitch-deck-builder` | Build pitch deck outline and narrative |
| `investor-outreach-drafter` | Draft personalized investor outreach |

## Outputs

- `funding-landscape-overview.md`
- `investor-prospect-list.md`
- `advisor-prospect-list.md`
- `pitch-deck-outline.md`
- `pitch-deck-narrative.md`
