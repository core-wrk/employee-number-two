# Project 02 — Brand Voice & Marketing Strategy

## Purpose

Translate market research into a coherent brand position and messaging framework. Define who the founder is talking to, what they are saying, and why it will resonate.

## Prerequisites

Complete Project 01 (market research).

## Global Context Files

**Loads:** `icp.md`, `competitive-landscape.md`, `startup-hypothesis.md`, `founder-profile.md`
**Writes:** `brand-voice.md`

## Skills

| Skill | Description |
|---|---|
| `positioning-strategist` | Articulate a clear positioning statement |
| `messaging-architect` | Build the messaging hierarchy |
| `brand-voice-developer` | Define brand personality and voice |

## Outputs

- `positioning-statement.md`
- `messaging-framework.md`
- `brand-voice-guide.md`
- `marketing-channel-strategy.md`
