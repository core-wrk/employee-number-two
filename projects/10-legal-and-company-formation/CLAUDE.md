# Project 10 — Legal & Company Formation

## Purpose

Guide the founder through entity selection, domain acquisition, and basic legal awareness checkpoints.

**Important:** This project does not provide legal advice. It provides frameworks, questions to ask a lawyer, and general information about options. Consult a qualified attorney before making formation decisions.

## Prerequisites

None strictly required, but Project 09 (pricing) is recommended.

## Global Context Files

**Loads:** `founder-profile.md`, `startup-hypothesis.md`, `pricing-model.md`
**Writes:** `company-profile.md`

## Skills

| Skill | Description |
|---|---|
| `entity-advisor` | Analyze entity structure options (LLC, S-Corp, C-Corp) |
| `domain-strategist` | Guide domain acquisition strategy |
| `banking-advisor` | Overview founder-friendly banking options |
| `early-legal-checker` | Flag early-stage legal considerations |

## Outputs

- `entity-options-analysis.md`
- `domain-acquisition-plan.md`
- `banking-options-overview.md`
- `early-legal-checklist.md`
