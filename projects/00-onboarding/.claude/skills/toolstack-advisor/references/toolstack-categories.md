# Toolstack Categories Reference

Internal reference for the `toolstack-advisor` skill. Organized by category with stage recommendations.

---

## Communication & Collaboration

**When relevant:** From day one if working with co-founders or early team members.

| Tool | Description | Free Tier | Best For |
|------|-------------|-----------|----------|
| Slack | Team messaging | Yes (limited history) | Teams of 2+ |
| Discord | Community + team chat | Yes | Community-first startups |
| Notion | Wiki, docs, project management | Yes (limited) | Solo founders or small teams |
| Linear | Issue tracking | Free for small teams | Product-focused teams |
| Google Workspace | Email, docs, calendar | No (but Google Docs is free) | Professional email presence |

---

## CRM & Sales

**When relevant:** Project 05 (Outbound Sales) or when the founder starts having sales conversations.

| Tool | Description | Free Tier | Best For |
|------|-------------|-----------|----------|
| HubSpot CRM | Full CRM | Yes (generous) | Founders who want structure early |
| Attio | Modern, flexible CRM | Yes (limited) | Startups wanting a clean interface |
| Folk | Lightweight relationship management | Yes (limited) | Early-stage relationship tracking |
| Google Sheets | Manual tracking | Yes | Pre-CRM stage |

**Adoption trigger:** When you are tracking more than 15-20 active conversations and a spreadsheet starts failing you.

---

## Email Marketing & Newsletters

**When relevant:** Project 04 (Inbound Marketing) when the founder has a waitlist or content strategy.

| Tool | Description | Free Tier | Best For |
|------|-------------|-----------|----------|
| Buttondown | Simple markdown newsletters | Yes (up to 100) | Minimalist founders |
| ConvertKit (Kit) | Creator-focused email | Yes (up to 1,000) | Content-driven startups |
| Mailchimp | Full email marketing | Yes (up to 500) | Founders who need automation |
| Loops | Modern email for SaaS | Yes (limited) | SaaS startups |

**Adoption trigger:** When you have a live signup page and are collecting email addresses.

---

## Website & Landing Pages

**When relevant:** Project 04 (Inbound Marketing) for waitlist pages; earlier if the founder wants a web presence.

| Tool | Description | Free Tier | Best For |
|------|-------------|-----------|----------|
| Carrd | Simple one-page sites | Yes (limited) | Quick landing pages |
| Framer | Design-forward websites | Yes (limited) | Design-conscious founders |
| Webflow | Full website builder | Yes (limited) | Founders who want more control |
| Next.js + Vercel | Code-based sites | Yes (generous) | Technical founders |
| Super.so | Notion-to-website | No | Founders already using Notion |

**Adoption trigger:** When you need a public-facing page — waitlist, landing page, or company site.

---

## Analytics

**When relevant:** Once you have a website or product that people are using.

| Tool | Description | Free Tier | Best For |
|------|-------------|-----------|----------|
| Plausible | Privacy-friendly analytics | No ($9/mo) | Founders who value simplicity |
| PostHog | Product analytics + feature flags | Yes (generous) | Product-led startups |
| Mixpanel | Event-based analytics | Yes (limited) | Data-driven founders |
| Google Analytics | Web analytics | Yes | Default option |

**Adoption trigger:** When you have traffic or users and need to understand behavior.

---

## Design

**When relevant:** Project 03 (Brand Identity) and whenever visual assets are needed.

| Tool | Description | Free Tier | Best For |
|------|-------------|-----------|----------|
| Figma | UI/UX design | Yes | Everyone — industry standard |
| Canva | Quick graphic design | Yes (generous) | Non-designers who need assets fast |
| Midjourney / DALL-E | AI image generation | Varies | Concept exploration, placeholder assets |

**Adoption trigger:** When you need visual assets — brand identity, pitch deck, marketing materials.

---

## Development & Deployment

**When relevant:** Project 07 (Product Development) or whenever building software.

| Tool | Description | Free Tier | Best For |
|------|-------------|-----------|----------|
| Vercel | Frontend deployment | Yes (generous) | Next.js / React apps |
| Railway | Backend deployment | Yes (limited) | Simple backend hosting |
| Supabase | Database + auth | Yes (generous) | Full-stack MVPs |
| Firebase | Backend-as-a-service | Yes (generous) | Mobile and web MVPs |
| AWS / GCP / Azure | Cloud infrastructure | Free tiers vary | Scaling past MVP |

**Adoption trigger:** When you start building the product.

---

## Payment & Billing

**When relevant:** Project 09 (Pricing and Packaging) or when ready to charge customers.

| Tool | Description | Free Tier | Best For |
|------|-------------|-----------|----------|
| Stripe | Payment processing | Pay per transaction | Default choice for most startups |
| Paddle | Payments + tax compliance | Pay per transaction | SaaS selling globally (handles VAT/sales tax) |
| LemonSqueezy | Simple digital product payments | Pay per transaction | Info products, simple SaaS |

**Adoption trigger:** When you are ready to accept payments.

| Tool | Description | Free Tier | Best For |
|------|-------------|-----------|----------|
| Clerk (for Stripe Atlas) | Incorporation | No ($500) | Delaware C-Corp formation |
| Mercury | Startup banking | Yes | Startup-friendly banking |
| Brex | Corporate cards | Yes (with qualifications) | Startups with funding |
